---

Deliver structured competitive and market research with clear takeaways.

## Workflow

1. Define scope:
   - Target market, competitors, and comparison criteria
   - Time horizon and regions (US/UK/EU, etc.)
2. Gather evidence:
   - Use public sources, document URLs, and summarize findings
   - Capture screenshots or key UX steps when requested
3. Synthesize:
   - Comparison tables, risks, and recommendations
   - Implications for product strategy or positioning

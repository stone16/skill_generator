Provide a fast, structured product kickoff that turns ambiguity into an initial plan.

## Workflow

1. Frame the problem:
   - Target users, pain points, business goals
   - Assumptions, constraints, and open questions
2. Establish scope and requirements:
   - Success criteria, MVP scope, key user stories
   - Risks and dependencies
3. Outline the initial plan:
   - Rough roadmap and milestones
   - Metrics/KPI tree and validation or research tasks

Synthesize product insights from qualitative and quantitative inputs.

## Workflow

1. Understand inputs:
   - Feedback corpus, usage metrics, or experiment results
   - Segment definitions and time windows
2. Analyze patterns:
   - Theme clustering, trend identification, and impact assessment
   - Segment comparisons and risk highlights
3. Deliver insights:
   - Summary of key findings with evidence
   - Recommended follow-up investigations or actions
